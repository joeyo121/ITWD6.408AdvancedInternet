Copyright, Creative Common (CC) Licenses, Fair Use

Basic Principles:

  • Copyright applies automatically when something is published.
  • The © symbol only acts as a reminder.
  • Copyright lasts up to 50 years.
Learn More: https://www.copyright.co.nz/understanding-copyright/what-is-copyright
Four types of CC licences and Fair Use:
  1. Attribution
  2. Share Alike
  3. No Derivative Works
  4. Non-Commercial
Learn More: https://creativecommons.org/about/cclicenses/

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Fives ways to improve visibility of a website on a search engine:

  1. Submit your website to Google Search Console.
  2. Submit your site to Google MyBusiness.
  3. Make sure your site loads fast, especially on mobile devices.
  4. Determine the right keywords.
  5. Create relevant backlinks to your site.

Web Performance & Maintenance

Five ways to reduce page loading time:

  1. Minify CSS, JavaScript, and HTML.
  2. Compress and optimize your images.
  3. Choose a performance-optimized hosting solution.
  4. Reduce your redirects.
  5. Cache your web pages.
Learn more: https://blog.hubspot.com/marketing/how-to-reduce-your-websites-page-speed
Five important actions/tasks to carry out when maintaining a website:
  1. Test Loading Speed.
  2. Fix Dead Links and Errors.
  3. Test Browser Compatibility.
  4. Publish Fresh Content.
  5. Make Regular Backups of Your Site.

Web Security

Five common cyber attacks and some preventative measures:

  1. Phishing Attacks
    Preventative: Scrutinize the emails you receive. Most phishing emails have significant errors like spelling mistakes and format changes from that of legitimate sources.
  2. Malware Attacks
    Preventative: Use firewalls. Firewalls filter the traffic that may enter your device.
  3. Man-in-the-Middle Attack
    Preventative: Be mindful of the security of the website you are using. Use encryption on your devices.
  4. Password Attack
    Preventative: Use strong alphanumeric passwords with special characters.
  5. Denial-of-Service Attack
    Preventative: Run a traffic analysis to identify malicious traffic.
Learn more: https://www.simplilearn.com/tutorials/cyber-security-tutorial/types-of-cyber-attacks
